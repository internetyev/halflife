## Product brief

**Concept:** User enters a job title → gets a dramatic obsolescence countdown, survival score, specific AI tools already disrupting the role, and a personalized pivot roadmap. Shareable per-role countdown cards. Annual "most at-risk roles" report as link bait.

**Viral mechanic:** Shareable countdown cards per role. LinkedIn native. Journalists covering AI will reference the annual report.

**AI layer:** Claude is the product — analysis, scoring, and pivot roadmap are all Claude API calls.
